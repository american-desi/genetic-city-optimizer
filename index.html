About

A multi-objective genetic algorithm optimizing city layouts on a 30×30 grid. Balances competing goals: minimizing commute distance, maximizing green space access, ensuring service coverage, managing noise and pollution.

Run for a few thousand generations and watch a city emerge from random noise into something that looks plausible — sometimes beautiful.
